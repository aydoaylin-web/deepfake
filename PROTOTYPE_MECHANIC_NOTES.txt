Deepfake Defender prototype. Normal gameplay logic

1. Voluntary feed review
Five posts in the scrolling feed display a Review button.
Players decide themselves whether to inspect a post.
They are not told in advance whether it is trustworthy or suspicious.

2. Push review
Concrete agency notifications still appear while scrolling.
The notification does not open automatically.
Players may open it or dismiss it.

3. Open analysis
Image analysis, source check, profile check, and comments are simultaneously available.
Each button reveals additional case-specific information.
No tool is mandatory.

4. Submission
Players choose Trustworthy or Suspicious.
They provide a written justification.
They submit a confidence rating from 1 to 5.

5. Scoring
A news review is correct only when both the verdict and the written justification are correct.
Confidence is recorded for research but does not change the points.
The task origin, feed or push, is also recorded.

6. Prototype content
The feed currently contains both suspicious and trustworthy review cases.
Push missions and voluntary feed reviews use the same core review interface.
